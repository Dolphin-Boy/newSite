What is PancakeSat
A new satellite form factor affordable to everyone: inexpensive and easy to use. The main tasks of this type of satellites are testing new equipment, studying physical phenomena and exploring space.
lightweight and affordable
The size of our device is 100 × 100 × 10 mm, its weight does not exceed 120 g. Thanks to the updated form factor, the cost of one PancakeSat does not exceed $ 500
Innovative and simple
More than 3 in-house technologies and developments are used in our project. Easy prototyping algorithm, simple architecture and innovative technologies will help to study space in more detail.
Compact and rational
The 1 / 10U size makes the new stacking technology available. 10 missions in one unit give you an opportunity to carry out the necessary experiments faster.

Support and problem solving
We carry out a full cycle of work on the satellite: from the conception of an idea to the launch of an apparatus into space. Besides manufacturing of the satellite itself and testing it, we undertake certification, launch, communication with the device and its maintenance.
To create a satellite suitable for even novice users, we redefined the form factor of the entire industry, especially the architecture of the device. We had to manufacture individual stabilization and undocking systems. The satellite motherboard has a power consumption of less than 0.1W in standard operating mode. To fit the full filling of the satellite into such a small body, nanotechnology and unique design solutions were used in the project!
